I am a highly motivated cybersecurity professional with a graduate-level focus on governance, risk management, and compliance (GRC). My academic foundation at the University of Dallas has equipped me with both theoretical depth and practical skills to address real-world cybersecurity challenges.

My work sits at the intersection of technical rigor and strategic thinking — I specialize in threat analysis, comprehensive risk assessments, and aligning organizational security postures with regulatory frameworks including NIST and ISO 27001.

Through hands-on academic projects and IT support experience, I have developed a strong ability to translate complex cybersecurity concepts into business-aligned solutions that protect critical systems and drive organizational resilience.

I am passionate about critical infrastructure protection, building security cultures, and contributing to a safer digital world.

Strategic Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

Governance, Risk & Compliance · Academic Project

Academic

Objective & Description

Tools & Frameworks
